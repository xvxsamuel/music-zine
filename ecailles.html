alcest
écailles de lune

there’s no point in writing about this album—you have to feel it. it's 41 minutes of pure bliss of ethereal, lush instrumentation that wraps around you like a warm hug after a cold day.

i wish i could capture what it feels like, but words fall short. all you can do is close your eyes, lie down, and let it speak to you in its gentle voice.

even if you think metal music isn’t your thing or you’ve never tried anything like it, i strongly recommend giving this album a chance.

released: 2010

genres: blackgaze, post-metal, shoegaze
